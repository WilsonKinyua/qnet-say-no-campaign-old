Get Involved

Join us in the collective effort to combat human trafficking, illegal migration, and fake job offers. You can do this by:
1. Spreading the Word:

Become an advocate for change by spreading awareness in your community. Share our Say NO! campaign materials, comic pamphlets and our other educational content on your social media platforms, workplaces, or local gathering spots.

2. Organizations Can Host Local Awareness Events:

Organize events in your community to raise awareness about the issues addressed by the campaign. Whether it's a workshop, seminar, or informational session, creating a local dialogue is key to fostering understanding.

3. Use Social Media:

Harness the power of social media to amplify the campaign's message. Use our designated hashtags, share our campaign updates, and encourage others to join the cause. Your online presence can contribute to a global movement.

4. Volunteer:

Consider volunteering your time and skills to support campaign initiatives. From organizing events to assisting with designing awareness campaigns, your involvement can make a tangible impact.

5. Collaborate with Organizations:

Forge partnerships with local NGOs, community groups, and other organizations that share a commitment to combating exploitation. Collaborative efforts strengthen the impact of the campaign.
